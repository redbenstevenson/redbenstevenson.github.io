About Gini Coefficient

The Gini coefficient measures income inequality within a country, ranging from 0 (perfect equality) to 1 (perfect inequality). In this game, values are multiplied by 100 for display.

A higher Gini coefficient means greater income inequality, while a lower value means more equal distribution of income.
